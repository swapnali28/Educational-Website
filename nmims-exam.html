Revised Pattern for NMIMS ODL Exams

OCTOBER 17, 2022

Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies is among the top 10 Business Schools in India. The university has received various accreditations by statutory bodies such as UGC, DEB and is also given category-1 status. It has created a hegemony in the management education based on the updated curriculum, placement services and a holistic education atmosphere.

NMIMS focuses on regular as well as distance courses so as to reach out to more students and help them to choose a mode that they are more inclined to. The university has been awarded category 1 status by UGC due to which they can tweak its curriculum according to the latest industry standards. Hence, it keeps revisiting their exam and study pattern.

NMIMS has recently made changes to their OLD Exam Pattern.

Earlier Pattern

1.Descriptive Questions – 20 Marks (attempt 2 out of 3)

2.MCQ – 50 Marks.

Revised pattern

1.Descriptive Questions – 30 Marks (attempt 3 out of 4)

2.MCQ – 40 Marks

  • 10 MCQ’s (0.5 marks each) = 5 marks
  • 15 MCQ’s (1 mark each) = 15 marks
  • 10 MCQ’s (2 marks each) = 20 marks

    • According to the university notice, the duration of the exam will remain same, that is, two and a half hours

    Note – The university has announced the news on the NMIMS Student Portal.
