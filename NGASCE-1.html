Exploring the Best Distance University for Management in India: NMIMS

The quest for new, better, and cost-effective means of delivering education and training is intensifying worldwide. Open and distance learning has gained tremendous popularity in the past few years. Studies have shown that by the year 2026, the market size is expected to reach INR 325.48 billion as compared to INR 91.41 billion in the year 2020. For those who were unable to complete their education for certain constraints or want to pursue further studies in any field, distance education gives them the chance to continue their studies or upgrade themselves with some professional course to get better career opportunities and land lucrative jobs.

What should you consider while choosing a university for ODL Mode?

Picking a university for a course is definitely not like picking chits in a game. Students should make their decision based on extensive research, expert advice and align their interests with that of the university’s. India has a huge number of universities that offer distance courses such as Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies, Amity University, Chandigarh University and many others. This article deals with why Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS) is superior to others. So, let’s dive in.

NMIMS Accreditations
  • NMIMS has been awarded Category 1 Autonomy by the UGC due to which the university can tweak its curriculum according to latest industry standards. They can also introduce and eliminate sections of the syllabus as per market needs.
  • The university programs are recognised by the UGC-DEB. This validates that the university’s courses maintain the higher education standards in the country.
  • The university is accredited by NAAC with an “A+” grade with 3.59 CGPA. Thus, testifying its academic excellence and infrastructural innovation.
  • It stands proud as a Deemed to be University offering multiple disciplines across multiple campuses and an integral contributor to the open and distance education market.
NMIMS Supremacy

NMIMS is among the top 10 Business Schools in India. The university aims to target working professionals to help them by giving a platform that is flexible and caters to all their needs. It has thoughtfully curated its curriculum and education system so as to make it easy for students to manage studies with their job. The university focuses on helping students maintain a smooth work-study-life balance.

Additionally, the university has been ranked amongst the top 100 business schools globally in the Financial Times Masters in Management Ranking in the year 2022. The university has received various other awards and rankings which justifies that it offers high quality education and is in line with global education standards. Additionally, it has introduced a unique exam pattern, excellent placement services and creative assignment procedure.

Unique Examination Pattern

The distance learning exam pattern at the university is unique and flexible. It is a robust evaluation system yet it offers flexibility to students to choose their date, time and subject according to their preference. It also allows the students to appear for examinations on weekends and provides them the option to appear for more than one subject on the same day to speed up the process.

Placement Services

The university provides excellent placement services. However, it offer two different placement options to the students to choose from, namely:

1. Alumni Portal

NMIMS Global provides free access to an “Alumni Portal.” This is where the students can look for jobs, network with alumni, corporate leaders and HRs of many renowned companies. The students get access to this portal after the end of 3rd semester where they can look at daily job postings.

2. Career Development Services

The university also offers paid “Career Development Services” for 12 months spread across two semesters that is 3rd and 4th. It consists of a mentor who helps students prepare for interviews and pays special emphasis on skill development. The Career Development Services are a five step module wherein the students are trained by an industry expert, get reviews on their academic profile and prepare for job interviews. Additionally, learning modules and career counseling is also an integral part of these services.

Benefits of joining NMIMS Open and Distance Learning Mode

Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies offers a wide range of programs and services with the aim to provide best-in-class education to the students that meets the latest industry standards and also helps students to compete in the global education market.

Following are some points that makes NMIMS stand out:-

  1. The university uses technology to the fullest to make learning simplified. It provides the students with a mobile and desktop-friendly application.
  2. Easy to use student portal that allows the students to access their course materials as well as other important notifications related to exams, assignments and much more.
  3. The distance education faculty is composed of scholar academicians and industry experts that actively help students grow professionally.
  4. The exams and assignments are evaluated using the robust evaluation method.
  5. The students gain access to both live and recorded lectures. Live lectures allow the students to communicate with their lecturers and classmates and the recorded lectures give them the opportunity to never miss their lecture session and also revisit for revisions.
Final Thoughts

NMIMS Global university has established itself as one of the leading universities designed for Open and Distance Learning. It uses all popular modern and traditional learning methods to give students the best learning experience. With flexibility in the education system it gives the students the opportunity to study along with their existing job schedules. Therefore, students gain adequate knowledge to meet their career goals.
