NMIMS Global Distance MBA Learning Programs

With the evolving and tech-based education system, NMIMS Global Distance MBA Programs cater to a huge chunk of students from around 300+ cities in India. Narsee Monjee is an established private Business School set up in 1981 and the evolution of the institute has been dynamic.

Considering the experience of the institute and how it has skillfully moved from paper-pen schooling to a full-fledged distance-based education system has attracted students in the past decade.

The University is entitled by UGC-DEB and is a member of AIU, it is endorsed and graded ‘A’ and 3.95 CGPA by NAAC. Given that, how the university understands the needs of a wide group of students it is also awarded Category 1 by UGC.

NMIMS Global Distance MBA programs are thoughtfully tailored according to the needs of working professionals. The mission behind offering distance MBA programs is that the professionals can learn and execute their learnings simultaneously, secondly, they can upskill while being in business or a day job.
