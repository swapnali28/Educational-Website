Will NMIMS GLOBAL Distance MBA be a good option with its fee structure?

Narsee Monjee Institute of Management (NMIMS) Global is among India’s top 10 Business Schools. The institute offers a variety of UG and PG programs in business administration, including Distance MBA, MBA (WX), and Distance UG & Diploma programs. All courses offered by NMIMS are approved and recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the Department of Distance Education (UGC-DEB) in India. The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) also certified NMIMS with an “A+” status grade and with a CGPA of 3.59 on a 4-point scale. Given its accreditations and what it has achieved so far, the institute stands on the list of the top 10 business schools in India.

Until so far, according to the statistics of the placements, the Highest CTC stood at 57.65 LPA whereas the Average CTC stood at 23.07 LPA. The highest salary has increased by 66.04% compared to the previous year. There was a participation of more than 150 recruiters in placement drives. Top recruiters included Aditya Birla Capital, American Express, Avendus, Crisil, Tresvista, and many more.

The flagship program-Master in Business Administration ( In distance Mode) offers 10 contemporary specializations. The program aims to develop leaders who may have the required domain knowledge, analytical skills, and decision-making skills in a rapidly changing world of an uncertain business environment. Students in their last year of bachelor’s degree/equivalent qualification examinations, as well as those who have finished degree prerequisites but are awaiting results, are eligible to apply for NMIMS distance MBA. The specializations offered by the college for open and distance learning programs in MBA are as follows: –

  • Business Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Financial Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • International Trade Management
  • Banking and Finance Management
  • Supply Chain Management
  • Operations Management
  • Retail Management
  • Information Technology and Systems Management

Is NMIMS Global Distance MBA fees justifiable?

NMIMS has thoughtfully crafted its course fee structure. They offer 3 types of courses within the distance MBA courses namely – Pro, Prime, and Prime+. Along with that they also offer placement assistance and free access to the Alumni Portal.

Keeping all the value additions in mind, the university has put forward a pocket-friendly fee structure in mind and at the same time competing very well in the education sector in India and perhaps, internationally.

NMIMS Distance MBA Fees Structure

Below is the fee structure for the distance MBA program for 2 years spread over 4 semesters: –

Payment-Options Prime Pro
One –time full fee payment Rs 1,20,000 Rs 1,44,000
Annual mode of payment Rs 65,500 Rs 77,500
Semester-wise fee payment Rs 35,000 Rs 41,000

NMIMS is one of the best institutes in India for distance MBA in terms of return on investment. The fee structure is fairly affordable for the quality of education offered. Being one of the best business schools in India, completing an open or distance MBA from NMIMS, opens up varied avenues for a candidate.

What are the USPs of the NMIMS Distance MBA course?

NMIMS Distance MBA helps working professionals to set up their timetable and schedule and their study time around family or work commitments. They provide various modules which can be repeated over and skipped if you are familiar with the concept, making the course easy to access. Students have the option of full payment, annual payment, or semester payment and will benefit from significant discounts in the first two payment modes.

The fee structure also depends on the type of lecture one chooses. Specifically, professional candidates choose for each semester. In Pro mode, students can access live interactive and recorded lectures, while in Prime mode, students can only access recorded lectures, but live lectures for individual subjects for a marginal fee of Rs 1,200.

The Program has a dedicated learning management portal that allows students to experience learning culture from anywhere. The structure of the program follows an updated curriculum of more than 200 faculty members, consisting of scholars and industry experts sought after, to ensure that students learn from the best.

What do students get within the fee structure of the distance course?

The Institute has partnered with leading companies to provide student interview opportunities, student support, and career support.

The fee structure is justifiable considering the below factors:

  1. Latest Online Learning Systems
  2. Dynamic, Application Oriented Assessment
  3. Best-in-class Student Services
  4. Career Services
  5. Free Alumni Portal
  6. University representation across the country.
  7. Great Placement History
  8. Support in the form of Email, calls, and also WhatsApp
  9. Guest Lectures from Industry Experts
  10. EMI and Loan Facility

Narsee Monjee, India’s premier institute, offers distance learning in business administration by industry leaders who teach students over two semesters. Not only does this feature help students drive exposure, but it also helps significantly in networking. In addition, NMIMS offers ”Career Advancement Services” that connect students to the most relevant work for a better transition. Career Assistance Services also offer the benefits of a comprehensive mock interview.

NMIMS has one of the best latest online learning systems. It has a Mobile application-based learning portal with facilities like recorded lectures, eBooks, and other resources. The institute also has great assessment orientations. There are computer-based exams at designated centres all over India. The university is best-in-class student services and multiple touchpoints; email, toll-free no., and live chat. A dedicated student support team to assist students. The students also have access to the university’s alumni portal where the students can find jobs for themselves, interact, and network with fellow students, seniors & recruiters and even land their dream job.

The institute offers great career service and career assistance with various programs. One such program is placement assistance which is a program primarily focused on skill development. The student in this program is assigned with a mentor who is not any faculty from the university but corporate personnel from the Industry working on a real-time basis with a good amount of experience in a particular domain.

NMIMS has recorded 100% placement assistance and is consistently ranked among the top B-schools in the country due to the various reasons mentioned above.

Overall, a candidate doesn’t have to worry about NMIMS charges, it’s worth every penny. It’s affordable owing to the facilities provided by the institute. It has the best-ranking place according to various ranking councils and statutory bodies. The institute overpowers many IIMs and other leading business schools in India because of the best facilities, career guidance, and quality education with a warranted fee structure.
